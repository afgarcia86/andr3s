## Who I Am
I am a product-minded engineer and technical leader who specializes in turning complex ideas into real, working products. My background blends design, software engineering, and systems architecture, and I enjoy solving difficult technical problems while keeping the user experience front and center. Today I serve as Lead Engineer at Harbor, where I helped turn an early concept into a product used by parents to reduce stress and get better rest.

## What Drives Me
I’m motivated by solving difficult problems and building systems that meaningfully improve people's lives. I especially enjoy the challenge of taking an idea from concept to reality—figuring out the architecture, prototyping quickly, and refining it into something reliable and scalable. I care deeply about building things well and creating products that feel thoughtful, intuitive, and genuinely useful.
